# Appendix: labs and market watch

This appendix is deliberately outside the core evidence base. These labs, companies, and programs may matter for landscape mapping, funding flows, talent clusters, and future source discovery, but they should not be used as evidence of scientific discovery performance unless linked to primary papers, reproducible benchmarks, repos, or experimental validation.

## Rule

Do not cite market-watch items as discovery evidence. Cite them only as:

- ecosystem context
- team / lab tracking
- funding or institutional signal
- source-discovery lead
