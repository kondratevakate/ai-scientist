# Contributing

This project is intended to remain a CC0 public-domain compilation of original
review work.

By contributing, you agree that your contribution is original or otherwise
clearly compatible with CC0, and that you waive copyright and related rights in
your contribution to the extent allowed by law.

Guidelines:

- Add source-traceable summaries, not copied third-party text.
- Link to papers, repositories, datasets, and official pages instead of
  embedding their content.
- Keep vendor claims separate from peer-reviewed or reproducible evidence.
- Preserve dates, query strings, source URLs, and uncertainty notes.
- Do not add local repository snapshots, model weights, datasets, PDFs, or raw
  database exports unless redistribution rights have been checked.
- Mark unresolved claims as tentative.
